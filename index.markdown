**Saeed Shahsavan** is a senior software engineer and software architect with more than 18
years of experience designing and implementing enterprise-scale distributed systems. Over
the course of his career, he has worked in ERP, telecommunications, government-to-citizen
platforms, and banking.
His expertise includes Go, Java, and React, with a deep focus on search engines, event-
driven architectures, and high-performance microservices. He has also served as a project
manager, technical lead, and instructor, mentoring teams on writing idiomatic, testable Java
and Go code and managing complex enterprise projects.
A lifelong reader of poetry and philosophy, he brings a thoughtful and human-centered
perspective to engineering, combining practical skills with architectural vision. He is
passionate about clarity, scalability, and performance, and aims to help professional
developers build maintainable systems while sharing lessons learned from real enterprise
projects.
